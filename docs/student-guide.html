Getting Started
Signing In
Sign in directly with your HKU email address and Student / Staff ID — no password, no email link needed.
⚠️
Only @hku.hk and @connect.hku.hk email addresses are accepted. Personal Gmail or other addresses will be rejected.
1
Go to hku-shanghai-forum.vercel.app and enter your HKU email address. Use your original HKU email (e.g. u36xxxxx@connect.hku.hk) — not one you may have changed or updated afterwards.
2
Enter your Student / Staff ID (e.g. 3035XXXXXX). Both fields are required before the Sign in button becomes active.
3
Click Sign in. Your credentials are verified instantly and you are redirected to your Dashboard.
💡
Your Student / Staff ID is the number on your HKU student card (e.g. 3035XXXXXX). If you cannot log in, contact your TA to confirm your ID is registered in the system.

02
Trip Preparation
Your Profile
Fill in your flight details, visa status, emergency contact, and health information. Your university info (name, faculty, student ID, etc.) is pre-filled by your TA from the class list — you cannot edit those fields.
✈️
Flight Information
Must complete
Ticket purchase status, departure flight (HKG → PVG), and return flight (PVG → HKG) — date, time, and flight number for each.
🛂
Visa Information
Must complete
Your current visa status (Not Started / In Progress / Approved / Not Required) and an optional notes field.
🆘
Emergency Contact Optional
Name, relationship, phone, and email of someone we can reach in an emergency.
🏥
Health & Dietary Optional
Any dietary restrictions or medical conditions your TA should be aware of. Leave blank if none.
ℹ️
Flight and visa fields count toward your profile completion %. Always tap Save Changes after editing.
03
Trip Milestones
Checkpoints
Confirm each milestone as you complete it. Your TA monitors these in real time to ensure everyone is safe and on track.
1
Open the Checkpoints page from the navigation menu.
2
Find the relevant checkpoint and tap Check in. The circle turns green with a checkmark to confirm.
3
Tapped by mistake? Tap Undo on a checked item to remove your check-in.

04
Issues & Incidents
Reports
Use reports to notify your TA of any problems, concerns, or requests during the trip. All reports are private between you and your TA.
1
Go to Reports in the navigation and tap New Report.
2
Enter a short Title (e.g. "Lost luggage at PVG") and a detailed Description with as much context as possible.
3
Tap Submit Report. Your TA will be notified and will reply as soon as possible.
4
Check your report for TA replies. The status updates as your TA handles it: Open → In Progress → Resolved.
5
If you submitted a report by mistake and it's still Open, you can tap Cancel Report from the report detail page.
🚨
Life-threatening emergencies — call local services first:
110 Police  ·  120 Ambulance  ·  119 Fire  (China mainland)
Then submit a report on the platform to alert your TA.

Course Information
Info Hub
All important trip and course information published by your TA, organized by category. Go to Info in the navigation to browse.

Use the category tabs at the top to filter by topic. Each block shows a title, body text, and optional clickable links. Your TA may add or update blocks throughout the trip — check back regularly.
